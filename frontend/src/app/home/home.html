The Unmatched Serenity of Parasnath

Rising 1,350 meters above the plains of Jharkhand, Parasnath Hill (Sammed Shikharji) is a majestic sanctuary of spirituality and natural beauty. It is the sacred ground where 20 out of the 24 Jain Tirthankaras attained Nirvana, making it a place of supreme reverence.

Whether you seek the spiritual grace of ancient marble shrines or the adventurous climb through dense deciduous forests, Parasnath offers a journey that purifies the soul and expands the mind.
